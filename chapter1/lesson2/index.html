If you haven't already, open main.js in your favorite text editor that supports syntax highlighting.
Don't have one? Download Sublime Text or VSCode.
Sublime Text is slightly easier to navigate as a first time user, and has a standalone version. VSCode on the other hand is an all-around better editor if that's the kind of thing you want to invest some time in learning.

Make sure you also have the console open in this window.
To open the console:
  • Right click anywhere on this page
  • Click "Inspect"
  • Click the "Console" tab in the window that opened

Now read main.js from the top to start your lesson!
